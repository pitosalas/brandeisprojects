name: OneCampus
blurb: Introducing the next generation of student-campus interaction, OneCampus. OneCampus combines all the necessary campus apps into one and displays them through the iconic DRIFT menu system comprising of campus dining, residential, finance, and tracking. The app envisions to replace the current plethora of campus-apps by providing APIs, and a full customizability to schools, students and every third party in between. The app also has an in-house team of SWEs to create individual features for colleges on-demand, and provides 24/7 customer service.
